The Ashenvale Mob is an Alliance Guild from the Nighthaven Realm on the Elysium World of Warcraft Vanilla 1.12 Server, the guild was founded sometime in 2018 by Acmeatom a Nightelf Hunter. The guild currently has 4 Officers and a total of 112 members. The officers are Yavannah a Nightelf Priest, Bandini a Nightelf Rogue, Olympos a Human Paladin and Falathiel a Nightelf Hunter.

The guild is not in a current state of raiding or PvP within the game, it is currently a friendly, leveling and helping guild that do all sorts from just chat, to actively help other members in the world, or even just running an instance or questing together. It is a very welcoming guild that are happy to help new players or new characters progress. To join you just simply need whisper the Guild Master, Acmeatom or the Officers and they'll invite you and your friends to join.

Even if you need help for some information in-game don't hesistate to ask I'm sure we'll be able to find what your looking for or find you a website with information that does or even recommend addons all you have to is ask.
The guild has a number of players that have a range of different proffesions that can help you with the items you need if you to be crafted for gear upgrades just ask and check the memberlist in-game for what proffessions they are and if they are online or not.

The guild also has a discord server that you can join and chat to us on.
